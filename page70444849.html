Your Apprenticeship at WAMOCON

Software Development from A to Z
Understanding concepts, analysing requirements, designing software, programming (Java, Python, JavaScript etc.), testing and implementing.
With us, you are not just an apprentice - from day one you are a full member of the team. We prepare you for the IT world of tomorrow - with exciting projects, cutting-edge technologies, and individual mentoring.
What You Will Learn and Do
Modern Technologies & Tools
You will work with Git, Jira, Confluence, Docker, Kubernetes (basics), cloud platforms, databases (SQL), APIs (Postman, Katalon), and get to know agile methods (Scrum, Kanban).
Project Work in a Team
Contributing to real client projects and internal development projects (e.g. the digital training system).
Creating test cases, running automated tests, and ensuring software quality (ISTQB foundations).
Quality Assurance & Testing
Databases & Data Management
Understanding and applying database technologies.
Troubleshooting & Support
Error analysis and problem resolution.
A special highlight of your apprenticeship: You play a key role in developing our innovative digital training system for future apprentice generations at WAMOCON. Here you can fully apply your creativity and knowledge in areas such as cloud architecture, AI-driven learning paths, and DevOps practices.

What You Bring
You don't need to be a coding expert to get started with us. What matters most is your enthusiasm and willingness to learn!
  • Großes Interesse an IT, Computern und Softwareentwicklung.
  • Logisches Denkvermögen und analytische Fähigkeiten.
  • High willingness to learn and self-motivation.
  • Teamfähigkeit und Kommunikationsstärke.
  • Zuverlässigkeit und sorgfältige Arbeitsweise.
Advantageous (but not essential)
  • Erste Programmiererfahrungen (z.B. durch Schulprojekte, AGs, private Projekte).
  • Erfahrungen mit Betriebssystemen und Office-Anwendungen.
